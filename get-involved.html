Q & A

Q: How can I adopt a pet from Pawsitive Futures?

A: Simply visit our “Adoption” page to browse available pets. Click on “Adopt” under the pet's profile and complete the form. Our team will contact you within 48 hours to schedule an interview and home visit.

Q: Are the animals fully vaccinated and healthy?

A: A: Yes! All our animals receive full medical checkups, are vaccinated, spayed or neutered, and come with detailed medical histories available on their profile pages.

Q: Can you help me choose the right pet for my home?

A: Absolutely. We take into account your lifestyle, household type, and experience level to help you find the best match for a successful adoption.

Q: What are the requirements to foster an animal?

A: Foster caregivers need a stable home, time to care for an animal, and availability to attend checkups. We provide all food, supplies, and support during the foster period.

Q: How do I become a volunteer?

A: Head to our “About Us” page and fill out the career application form. We'll schedule an orientation and provide any training needed based on your interests.

Q: Do I need experience to volunteer?

A: Not at all! We welcome anyone with a caring heart. Basic training is provided for tasks like feeding, cleaning, and enrichment activities.

Q: How is my donation used?

A: Your contributions go toward food, veterinary care, housing, and outreach efforts. We publish annual reports to maintain full transparency on how funds are used.

Q: Can I donate to a specific animal?

A: Yes, you can choose to make a targeted donation. Just note the animal's name or project in the donation form and we'll allocate the funds accordingly.

Q: Do you accept in-kind donations like food or toys?

A: Yes! We accept unopened pet food, clean bedding, toys, and other useful supplies. Visit our “Support Us” page for a list of needed items.
